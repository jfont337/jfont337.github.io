Ministry in california

During the summer of 2021, I went out to counsel at a Christian camp for the first time. I went back in the summer of 2022, and I have grown to love the ministry opportunities that I've been given. Both summers I have counseled 4-6th graders. I had the opportunity to share the gospel with many of them and point them to the hope that they can have in Christ. 

Future plans

My hope is to move out to California this summer, and be able to participate in this camp ministry for at least the next year. I am excited about the opportunities that await as I continue to minister to people in California through the camp, as well as through the church I attend out there. 

A need for the gospel

California truly is a beautiful place, but it has many spiritual needs. Many people make jokes about California and shy away from even thinking about moving there. Understandably so, but California needs to be reached with the gospel. The harvest is plentiful, but the laborers are few.
